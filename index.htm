About CCI Projects Pvt. Ltd.

The establishment of Cable Corporation of India Ltd (CCI). is as old as electric cable manufacturing in India. CCI was founded back in 1957; when two Indian business groups, Thackerseys and Khataus, joined hands with two German companies, Siemens and F&G to commence a PVC power cable factory, that manufactured PVC insulated and sheathed cables and wires for the first time in India.
